Who is Cash Compton?

I am a perfectionist who loves building things. I will work tirelessly to finish complex projects because I love what I do and I take pride in my work. In my opinion, there is no better feeling than getting a difficult program to work after you've been working non-stop for hours on end.

When I'm not on the computer, I enjoy being active. I usually go for a run 6 days a week. My biggest passion is soccer, which I spend a lot of time playing and watching. And finally, when I need a break, I like to take a much-needed nap.
